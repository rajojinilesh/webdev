Telco Churn Analysis (EDA)

This project focuses on understanding the factors influencing customer churn through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA). The dataset contains information about 7,043 customers, including their demographics, subscription details, and usage of telecom services. Senior Citizens have a 42% churn rate, compared to 22% for non-senior citizens. Fiber optic Internet users have the highest churn rate at 42%, compared to 18% for DSL users. Customers without value-added services like OnlineSecurity and TechSupport are more likely to churn.
